Development & Learning
Continually Working to Improve my Skills

In my journey of continuous education, the pursuit of a Master of Science in Computer Science has been a cornerstone of my professional development. The coursework that has most profoundly influenced my approach and understanding includes Database Management, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing Security. These classes have offered hands-on assignments that have not only enhanced my technical skills but also deepened my theoretical knowledge.

One of my standout projects involved creating an Azure SQL database from the ground up, tailored to the needs of a hypothetical medical company. This assignment allowed me to apply my understanding of database structures, queries, and management in a practical context, cementing my interest in and knowledge of cloud-based database solutions. Another significant endeavor was in the field of machine learning, where I delved into Python programming and trained multiple models to perform specific tasks. This experience opened my eyes to the potential of machine learning in solving real-world problems and its applicability across various industries.

Alongside my academic pursuits, I have obtained multiple certifications to bolster my expertise and demonstrate my commitment to my career in IT. The CompTIA ITF+ certification validated my foundational IT skills, the Microsoft Azure Fundamentals and Azure AI Fundamentals certifications showcased my proficiency within the Azure cloud environment, and the Certified Scrum Master (CSM) certification broadened my understanding of agile project management practices. These certifications have not only enriched my skill set but also highlighted my dedication to adopting best practices in IT and project management.

Looking ahead, I am eager to further deepen my mastery of the Azure environment and explore additional cloud architectures. My future learning objectives are squarely aimed at enhancing my contributions to the organizations I am part of. By staying abreast of the latest technological advancements and continuously expanding my technical and leadership skills, I aim to drive innovation and efficiency in my future roles, ensuring that my work has a lasting impact on the success and growth of my team and the broader organization.
