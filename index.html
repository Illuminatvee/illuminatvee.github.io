About

IT Engineer

I'm an IT Engineer with 4 years of experience across endpoint management, cloud infrastructure, identity and access management, and Microsoft 365 administration. I work best in environments where I can own projects end to end, from planning and engineering through rollout and documentation.

In my current role at Patriot Growth Insurance Services, I've led infrastructure modernization including rebuilding Azure Virtual Desktop environments to Entra ID-joined and Intune-enrolled infrastructure, designing Windows 365 Cloud PC compliance policies, and driving Windows 10 to 11 upgrades org-wide. I've also supported M&A integration work spanning email and data migrations, led SharePoint migrations, and drove company rebranding efforts involving domain transitions and API-driven account automation across 200+ office locations.

Outside of work I spend my time kayaking Florida's freshwater springs, wrangling Nyx (Belgian Malinois), Mildred, and Winifred (miniature dachshunds), and keeping 8 opinionated chickens happy.
